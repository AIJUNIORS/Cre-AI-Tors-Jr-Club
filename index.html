How it works

One lesson a week.
Six days of discovery.

Every week follows the same rhythm — so children always know what to expect. Consistency builds confidence.

1Teach📖
2Guided✏️
3Unplug🎲
4Explore🔭
5Create🛠️
6Share💬
7Reflect⭐

Day 1 is always a new idea

A story, a demo, a question that sparks curiosity. Never a lecture.

Days 2–6 are all about doing

Guided practice, physical games, digital tools, creative building, sharing with others.

Day 7 is honest self-reflection

Not a test. A conversation — what did I learn? What do I want to know next?

Ethics in every single week

One question, every lesson, connecting technology to the humans who build and use it.
