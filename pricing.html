Questions

Common questions

What is included in each level? ▼
Each level includes the full 50-week curriculum, weekly workbooks, lesson plans, all project resources, and Google Classroom materials. Pixel Blockers, Minecrafters, and Unicorns include the entry assessment quiz. Unicorns includes the Young AI Pioneer graduation certificate.
Do I need a tutor or can we do this at home? ▼
Both work well. Many families work through levels together at home — self-paced, one lesson per week. If you prefer guided support, our tutors deliver sessions via Google Meet and track progress in Google Classroom. School and group delivery is also available — contact us for details.
What devices and tools do we need? ▼
A tablet, laptop, or desktop with internet access. All tools used — Scratch, ScratchJr, Teachable Machine, ML4Kids, and Google Classroom — are completely free. No additional software purchases needed.
Is there a refund policy? ▼
Since Nano Tots is free, we encourage every family to start there before purchasing. If you purchase a level and are not satisfied within the first two weeks, contact us and we will work something out. Our goal is that every child succeeds.
Can my child skip levels? ▼
Yes — that is exactly what the entry quiz is for. Take the quiz for any level and if your child demonstrates strong understanding of the prerequisite concepts, they can start at that level directly. Age is a guide, not a gate.
How long does each level take? ▼
Each level is 50 weeks — roughly one school year at one lesson per week. Families working faster (2 lessons per week) can complete a level in about 6 months. Access is lifetime once purchased.
Is there a group or school discount? ▼
Yes — we work with schools and community groups at custom rates. Contact us via the About page and we will respond within 48 hours.
