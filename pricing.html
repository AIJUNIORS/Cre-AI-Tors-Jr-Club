Questions

How it works

Does my child get access immediately? ▼
Nano Tots is open to every child right away — no contribution needed. For other levels, access is confirmed once your contribution is agreed and underway. Contact us and we will sort it together, usually within a few days.
What does volunteering actually involve? ▼
Volunteering means actively participating in creAItors Club sessions for a minimum of two weeks. This could be assisting a tutor during a class, helping with session logistics, supporting content creation, or helping run a community event. We will agree the specifics with you before you begin.
What kind of content can I create? ▼
Content covers one week of our curriculum — explaining a concept, walking through an activity, or demonstrating what children learn. We review all content before it goes live and will give you a clear brief. Your name and contribution are credited on the video.
How does community referral work? ▼
Tell a family about creAItors Club and let us know you referred them. When they get in touch and join the community, your referral is confirmed. Both families benefit — you for bringing them, them for joining through someone they trust.
Can I combine contribution types? ▼
Absolutely. Families who contribute consistently across multiple forms become valued long-term community members — and we recognise that in how we work with you. Get in touch and we will have a conversation about what makes sense.
What if our situation is complicated? ▼
Just contact us. We will have a real conversation. creAItors Club exists to make AI literacy accessible — we will always find a way to make it work for families who are genuinely committed to being part of the community.
